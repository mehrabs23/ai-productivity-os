# Example: YouTube Creator Productivity System

## Building a Personal Productivity System with AI — Example Systems

---

**Persona:** Priya, 27 — part-time YouTube creator (personal finance niche), full-time bank associate, 22K subscribers, posting 2x per month

**Goal:** Grow to 100K subscribers and monetise channel to replace side-hustle income within 12 months.

---

### Priya's Situation at the Start of the Course

- Uploads 2 videos per month but feels like she's always running late on scripts
- Video ideas scattered across WhatsApp notes, a Google Doc, and her memory
- Zero system for audience research, thumbnail testing, or community management
- Spends 90% of her creative time on production, 0% on strategy or distribution
- Her biggest problem: she can't tell whether any given video performs well until it's been live 3 weeks

---

### What She Built

---

#### Goals Database

| Goal | Success Criteria |
|------|-----------------|
| Reach 100K subscribers by December | YouTube Studio: 100,000 subscribers shown. Not close — actual milestone needed: 50K by June |
| Generate £2,000/month from channel | Channel monetised. Average monthly revenue from CPM + affiliates ≥ £2,000 for 3 consecutive months |
| Publish 2x per month consistently | 24 videos published in 12 months. Maximum 1 missed month. |

---

#### Projects Database

| Project | Linked Goal | Priority | Notes |
|---------|------------|---------|-------|
| Q1 Content Pipeline | Publish 2x/month consistently | High | 6 videos planned for Jan–Mar. Scripts for Jan finalized. |
| Channel Distribution | Reach 100K subscribers | High | Identify 3 cross-promotion channels: Instagram, Reddit, TikTok |
| Monetisation Setup | £2,000/month revenue | Medium | Affiliate partnerships: 2 finance tools. Amazon Storefront. |

---

#### Priya's Milestone Map (Content Pipeline)

| Milestone | Target Date | Status |
|-----------|------------|--------|
| 6-video content plan finalised | Jan 8 | ✅ Done |
| Script 1 completed and recorded | Jan 15 | ✅ Done |
| Editing complete for Video 1 | Jan 20 | 🟡 In Progress |
| Video 1 published with SEO + thumbnail | Jan 25 | ⬜ Not Started |
| Script 2 completed | Jan 28 | ⬜ Not Started |

*"Working backwards from the Jan 25 publish date, I need editing done by Jan 20 which means recording by Jan 15. I can't start recording until the script is 100% done — that's the constraint."*

---

#### Attention Budget: Priya's Reality Check

| Domain | Ideal | Actual (Week 1) | Gap |
|--------|-------|----------------|-----|
| Content Production | 40% | 65% | -25% |
| Strategy / Ideas | 20% | 5% | +15% |
| Distribution / Community | 20% | 0% | +20% |
| Admin / Work | 15% | 30% | -15% |
| Learning (platform/trends) | 5% | 0% | +5% |

**AI Observation from the Attention Budget Prompt:**  
*"Distribution is receiving zero attention despite being named in your second-highest priority project. Strategy ideation is also almost absent. You're in full production mode — which produces videos but not an audience."*

**Priya's one change from this analysis:**  
> Protect Friday 7–9pm for strategy and distribution work. No production on Fridays.

---

#### Her Daily Planning Routine

Every Monday morning before opening any social media:
1. Open Notion dashboard
2. Identify what must be done in content production this week
3. Run the Morning Planning Prompt with: available hours (evenings only — 2hrs/day weekdays, 8hrs Saturday)
4. Block Saturday as a deep production day (minimum 6 hours)
5. Save plan to Monday's Daily Note

---

#### Daily Check-In Extract (Real entry from Day 3 of the course)

**What I completed:**
- Finished Script 2 rough draft (1,500 words)
- Scheduled 3 Reddit posts for this week's distribution experiment
- Replied to 17 comments from January video

**What didn't move:**
- SEO research for Video 2 thumbnail — avoided (3rd day in a row)

**Honest reflection:**  
*The thumbnail avoidance is a pattern. I don't feel confident about SEO. I'm going to use the task breakdown prompt to identify exactly what I don't know, then decide whether to learn it or delegate it.*

---

#### Capstone Highlights

**Most proud of:** The content pipeline milestone map. For the first time, she can see that missing a script deadline by 3 days pushes the publish date back by 5 days due to editing handoff.

**Would change:** "I added 6 sections to my dashboard and I'm only actually looking at 2 of them. I'm going to remove everything except Today's Focus and This Week."

**Unexpected finding from attention budget:** "I've been spending 30% of my creative-work hours on admin — emails, invoice chasing, responding to brand deals. That's time I said I was spending on YouTube."

---

### Prompt Sequence Priya Used

1. **Goal Definition Prompt** → Found out her "100K" goal didn't have a meaningful intermediate milestone
2. **Milestone Generation Prompt** → Broke content pipeline into filming/editing/publishing checkpoints
3. **Reverse Planning Prompt** → Confirmed her January publish date requires an unrealistic script-to-edit turnaround (solved by moving publish to 28th)
4. **Attention Budget Prompt** → Revealed distribution black hole
5. **Daily Planning Prompt** → Saved 20 minutes per Monday; replaced chaotic "I'll figure it out" approach
6. **System Retrospective Prompt** → Stripped dashboard from 6 sections to 2; added SEO learning task to Friday block
