### When to Run This

Run this retrospective:
- **Every 4 weeks** (set a recurring calendar reminder)
- **After a major disruption** (holiday, intense project sprint, life event that broke your routines)
- **When you notice your system is being used less than it was** (the moment you open Notion and feel resistance)
