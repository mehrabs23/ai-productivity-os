# Day 3 Completion Checklist

## Building a Personal Productivity System with AI

**Use this checklist at the end of Day 3 class to verify your Life OS is operational.**

This is not a grading sheet — it is a self-honesty check. If an item is not complete, make a note of what is needed and do it before tomorrow's class.

---

## Part A: Life OS Workflow and Kanban

- [ ] I have one unified set of workflow statuses across all my tasks: Inbox / Planned / This Week / In Progress / Waiting / Done
- [ ] I have a Kanban board view that shows tasks from at least 2 different life areas (not just work)
- [ ] I have an "Active Work" filtered view on my Kanban (Inbox and Done excluded)
- [ ] I have moved my real tasks into their correct status columns (not just defaults)
- [ ] I have set a WIP limit (maximum 3 tasks in "In Progress" at once)

---

## Part B: Life OS Dashboard

- [ ] I have a single Notion page with an icon, cover, and full-width layout
- [ ] I can see today's most important task within 5 seconds of opening the dashboard (5-Second Test passed)
- [ ] My dashboard has at least 5 linked database views
- [ ] My dashboard shows tasks from more than one area of my life (not just work)
- [ ] I have set this page as my Notion home

---

## Part C: Due Date Visibility and Reminders

- [ ] At least 5 of my tasks have Due Dates set
- [ ] I have a "Due Soon" view showing tasks due in the next 3 days
- [ ] I have an "Overdue" view showing tasks where due date has passed (and Status ≠ Done)
- [ ] Both views are visible on my dashboard
- [ ] I have set Notion reminders on at least 2 tasks
- [ ] I have a Reminder Date property on my Task database

---

## Part D: Daily Check-In

- [ ] I have a Daily Check-In section at the top of my dashboard
- [ ] The check-in includes at least 3 meaningful questions (not just "what am I doing today")
- [ ] I have completed today's check-in for real — not as a test, but as actual use
- [ ] The check-in takes less than 5 minutes to fill in
- [ ] I can explain to someone else the purpose of a daily check-in in one sentence

---

## Part E: Weekly Status

- [ ] I have a Weekly Status section at or near the bottom of my dashboard
- [ ] I have a "Completed This Week" linked view working correctly
- [ ] I have a written status template with at least 3 prompt lines
- [ ] I have partially filled in this week's status

---

## Part F: Overall Usability

- [ ] I have one place to see what matters today (not three tabs, two apps, and a sticky note)
- [ ] I can see which tasks are overdue without having to search
- [ ] I can see which tasks are coming up in the next 3 days
- [ ] I can see real progress from this week in my "Completed Recently" or "Completed This Week" view
- [ ] I can explain what my Life OS status is right now, in under 2 minutes, to someone else
- [ ] My dashboard feels useful — not just structured

---

## End-of-Day Reflection

Answer these three questions before closing Notion today:

1. **What is the most important task I will do first thing tomorrow?**

2. **What does my Overdue view tell me that I need to acknowledge?**

3. **What one change would make my dashboard more useful tomorrow morning?**
