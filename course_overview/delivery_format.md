# Delivery Format

## Building a Personal Productivity System with AI

---

### Overview

This course is designed for **live, instructor-led delivery** over four consecutive days. Each day is a 5-hour working session structured as a rhythm of short instruction bursts, live demos, and extended hands-on practice.

The course materials are also fully compatible with:
- **Asynchronous self-paced learning** (learner works through each day independently)
- **Hybrid delivery** (async video instruction + live coaching sessions)
- **Workshop format** (condensed 2-day intensive with longer sessions)

### Teaching Method: Show-Then-Build

Each session follows a three-part teaching pattern:

**1. Concept Introduction (5–10 min)**  
The instructor explains the concept clearly and briefly. Why does this matter? What problem does it solve? The explanation uses concrete examples from the learner personas (YouTube creator, student, freelancer).

**2. Live Demo (10–15 min)**  
The instructor builds the feature live in Notion or demonstrates the ChatGPT prompt in real-time. This is always done with a fictional learner persona's system, not the instructor's personal data.

**3. Student Task (25–35 min)**  
Learners build the same component in their own system, using their own goals and projects. The instructor circulates (live) or is available on a support channel (async). A structured task sheet guides every step.

### Platform Requirements

#### Live Delivery
- Video conferencing with screen share (Zoom, Google Meet, or equivalent)
- Shared Notion template link (duplicated at start of Day 1)
- Shared ChatGPT prompt library document (linked from course materials)
- Optional: collaborative whiteboard (Miro, FigJam) for group brainstorming

#### LMS / Leapiq Deployment
- Course materials are structured for import as markdown content
- Videos (instructor recordings) slot into the Teaching portion of each session
- Student task sheets serve as guided assignment documents
- Reflection templates are used as journal/assignment submissions

### Session Pacing Philosophy

This course assumes learners are working on **real goals and real projects**. This means:

- Some learners will be faster (they know exactly what their goals are)
- Some learners will be slower (they are discovering gaps in their own thinking for the first time)

The instructor should **hold time estimates loosely**. The task sheets are designed so that a fast learner can go deeper on any task, and a slower learner still completes the minimum viable version.

Each session includes **Minimum Viable Task** (must complete) and **Extension Tasks** (for those who move fast).

### Technical Setup for Learners

Before Day 1, learners should:

1. Create a free [Notion](https://notion.so) account
2. Create a free [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) account
3. Bookmark the course repository or access the LMS course page
4. Duplicate the Notion template (link provided by instructor at course start)
5. Download or bookmark the student task sheets for Day 1

> **Instructor note:** Allow 15 minutes at the start of Day 1 for tech setup troubleshooting. Most issues are account creation or browser compatibility.

---

### Group Exercises and Discussion

Three types of group interactions are embedded throughout:

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Pair share** | 2–3 per day | Learners share their goal/project structure with a peer for feedback |
| **Discussion prompt** | 1–2 per day | Structured question for whole-group or small-group discussion |
| **Share-out** | End of each day | 2–3 volunteers share what they built and why |

All discussion prompts are curated in [`instructor_guide/discussion_questions.md`](../instructor_guide/discussion_questions.md).

---

### Assessment

The course uses a **deliverable-based assessment model** rather than quizzes or exams:

- Each day has a defined set of required deliverables
- The final capstone is a 10-minute presentation of the learner's complete system
- Full details in [`assessment_and_capstone.md`](assessment_and_capstone.md)
