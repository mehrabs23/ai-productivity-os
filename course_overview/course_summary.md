# Course Summary

## Building a Personal Productivity System with AI

### One-Line Description
A 4-day, hands-on course where learners build their own AI-assisted personal productivity operating system using Notion and ChatGPT — no coding required.

---

### Course At a Glance

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Title** | Building a Personal Productivity System with AI |
| **Duration** | 4 days × 5 hours = 20 hours total |
| **Format** | Live instruction + guided practice + daily deliverables |
| **Level** | Beginner-friendly; no technical background required |
| **Tools** | Notion (free), ChatGPT (free tier), Jupyter/Colab (optional extension) |
| **Outcome** | A fully functional, personalised AI productivity system |

---

### Course Premise

Most professionals have three problems:
1. They know what matters, but don't have a system to track it
2. They feel busy, but can't show meaningful progress
3. They try tools, but abandon them within two weeks

This course solves all three — not by teaching another framework, but by helping each learner build a **personalised system** that fits their actual life, using AI to do the heavy lifting.

---

### The Approach

This course is built on three principles:

**1. Build Before You Understand**  
Learners build their Notion system on Day 1, and refine their understanding across the rest of the course. This avoids the "I'll start once I understand everything" trap.

**2. Prompts as Thinking Partners**  
ChatGPT is used not as a shortcut, but as a structured thinking partner. Every AI interaction is guided by a curated prompt from the course prompt library.

**3. Reflection as Infrastructure**  
Streaks, daily check-ins, and weekly reviews are not extras — they are core infrastructure. By Day 4, learners have already practiced reflection three times.

---

### Day-by-Day Arc

| Day | Theme | Core Deliverable |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| **Day 1: Foundation** | Goals, projects, and tasks | Notion workspace with goals, projects, and task database |
| **Day 2: Prioritization** | Milestones, planning, and effort allocation | Prioritized milestone map + attention budget |
| **Day 3: Dashboard & Tracking** | Kanban, progress visibility, and usability | Working productivity dashboard with streak tracker |
| **Day 4: AI Review & Capstone** | AI planning loop, reflection, and retrospective | Daily AI planning workflow + capstone presentation |

---

### What Learners Build (Cumulatively)

By the end of the course, each learner will have assembled a complete productivity operating system consisting of:

- A **Goals Database** with success criteria and AI-generated definitions
- A **Projects Database** linked to goals, with milestones and reverse-planned timelines
- A **Task Database** with priority categories, deadlines, and status tracking
- An **Anti-To-Do List** capturing completed work and preventing to-do list guilt
- A **Work Session Log** for tracking actual effort across projects
- An **Attention Budget** for comparing desired vs. actual focus allocation
- A **Kanban Board** with customised workflow stages
- A **Master Dashboard** with linked views, streak tracker, and progress indicators
- A **Daily AI Planning Routine** using a structured prompt sequence
- A **Weekly Review Ritual** using guided reflection questions

---

### Prerequisites

None. Learners need:
- A free Notion account
- A free ChatGPT account
- A computer with internet access
- Willingness to be honest about where their time actually goes

---

### Teaching Model

This course is designed for live delivery with an instructor. However, all materials are structured for asynchronous use as well. Each session includes:
- A short teaching portion (concept introduction)
- A guided demo (instructor builds in real time)
- A student task (learner builds their own version)
- A brief group share-out or discussion
